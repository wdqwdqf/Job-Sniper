About Job Sniper Talent Acquisition

Job Sniper Talent Acquisition is a well-established employment agency based in Nepal. We provide better employment opportunities and strive to grow with the help of technology. Our mission is to promote equal opportunities, ensure transparency, and provide easy access to more jobs through a well-designed website.

Our Vision

We envision a world where job opportunities are accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or location. Our goal is to make our services available to a broader demographic by leveraging technology.

Features of Our Job Portal

  • Secure and effective authentication system with registration, login, and logout functionality.
  • Display all the job listings with proper pagination.
  • Ability to add, update, and delete jobs by their respective logged-in companies.
  • Job application listings on respective companies' dashboard pages.
